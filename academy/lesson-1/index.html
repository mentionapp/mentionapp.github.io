About Media Monitoring 101

Media Monitoring 101 is an 8-week email course that will teach you how to use media monitoring to grow your business. We’ll show you how to apply it to all kinds of business functions: from marketing and PR to sales and support.

You’ll hear from all kinds of different professionals about how they use monitoring to make their job (and life) easier. They’ll share their success stories, yes, but also expect deep dives into how they use monitoring to achieve success, leaving you with a roadmap to follow for your own business.
